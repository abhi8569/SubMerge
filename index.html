How It Works

📤

Upload

Drop multiple .srt or .vtt files. The app detects language from filenames automatically.

🔗

Auto-Pair

Files with the same base name are paired together. Swap or adjust timing offsets as needed.

⚡

Merge

Cues are matched by time overlap and merged into a single dual-language subtitle file.
